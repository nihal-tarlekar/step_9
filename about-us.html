ABOUT US

Bringing the best of skills and resources in one place.

We have worked in the service industry for the past 13 years, delivering delicious meals to industries, baking the best bread, and serving energising coffee. Being connected with corporates for these many years has informed us of one tedious challenge they face—managing multiple contacts and vendors for specific tasks. We wanted to end that.

Step9 was started to end the hassle of finding good service and vendors and keeping abreast of updates from multiple sources. Now, you have one point of contact for all ancillary services.

Upgrading security at your premises with robust security systems and qualified personnel.

Serving delicious meals in convenient meal boxes.

Maintaining healthy, hygienic, and clean spaces consistently.

CORE VALUES

At the heart of our every service is quality, customers, and simplicity.

Quality

Our procedures are digitised for proper management and better customer service. Rigorous background checks are done before hiring, and SOPs are followed for minimal impact on the environment.

Customers

Providing value to customers is vital for us. We do that by providing tailored services, reducing their burden, and having flexible pricing options.

Simplicity

Step9 was born out of the need to simplify an essential and complex service issue for businesses. We want to simplify accessing the ancillary services so that you can spend more time on your primary services.
